WHAT WE STAND FOR:

Climber Engineers, Climber Enviro Solutions and Climber Systems is an umbrella organization built on the foundation of innovation. We are driven by a purpose that is 25 years young and it lies at the center of all our thoughts and plans that drive our actions. We look at actions and solutions that are sustainable and lead to ‘Caring for Life’. We aim to tap a large reservoir of entrepreneurial energy in India by investing in breakthrough ideas and embracing entrepreneurship. We plan to build an economic model of growth so India will be able to unleash the power of innovation to ensure a better life for the Rural Indian community. India is where our roots are and we want to make a difference to the lives of Rural Indian community with innovation and create new paradigms in machine design and manufacture. We are strong advocates of ‘Make in India’ and ‘Innovate in India’ to committed to fulfill the promise of ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat’.

Companies and Products:

Climber Engineers

Based on the principles and background of industrial automation, climber engineers aim towards development of technologies, R&D, in various fields of profession including industrial automation, special purpose machines, acoustic treatment of plants and safety enclosures.

Climber Systems

Climber systems has been actively working into design and development of machinery for agricultural field, to increase production efficiency during and post harvesting along with customized agricultural solutions for tools as needed. The company has been affiliated under ISO standards of manufacturing and has been associated with institutes such as MCCIA; Vigyan Ashram, India.

Climber enviro-solutions

Enviro-solution believes its aim in the field of sanitation and waste management, considering both organic and inorganic waste. With products ranging from Modular and sustainable bio toilets to reactors for converting plastic to fuel and much more. The company has been associated with AIT, Thailand; Bill and Melinda gates foundation, Pune University, India and many more institutions leading in the field
