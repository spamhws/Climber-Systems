Our Story

The Beggining We embarked on our journey by making a 5 HP agricultural Power Tiller. We were the first generation entrepreneurs and even though we had the knowledge, we didn’t have resources to fulfill our dream.In those days Agriculture was done in a traditional way and the farming community was not aware about the benefits of automation. Thus our first project received a lukewarm response but we didn’t lose hope.

1996

Climber Engineers During that period we had an opportunity to work in sectors such as Pharmaceutical, Agriculture, Automation and Packaging. Until 2006, we only designed the machines; soon we began to manufacture them under the aegis of Climber Engineers which made customized machine design, manufacture and installment.

2006

To work towards offering customized design solutions to individual customers, a separate entity named Climbers systems was formed. We had an opportunity to work on a project initiated by the Bill Gates Foundation. They had asked over 70 institutes from all over the world to ‘Re-invent the toilet’. One of those institutes was Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand which had developed a technology based on the conditions set by the foundation; not to dig the soil, should be off-grid, reuse water and the effluents must be pathogen and odor free. However they needed someone to make the live model and approached us to make it. We made it in only 7 months which was a achievement and they were impressed by our work. The Head of Bill Gates Foundation, Bangladesh, Roshan Shreshtha, their Publicity Head Din Cattsand the head of world sanitation Dulai Kone visited our plant in Katraj, Pune. They wanted to meet us to confirm that such technology could be transformed in to reality.

2013

The Re-invented toilet that we built was exhibited in an Expo of Bill Gates Foundation and our name became significant as a company that can build a toilet which the Indian rural community needs most. Then we thought to ourselves; why not make it on a large scale? Thus we became the link between the technology and the end user of it.

2014

Climber Enviro Solutions We realized that the sanitation requirements of the Indian rural community needed customized toilets which could be placed anywhere and which were mobile. To fulfill this need, we formed a separate company called Climber Enviro Solutions works in the sanitation as well as waste management sector. Plastic waste is one of the biggest threats to our environment. We built a machine by using a technology and produced Diesel from it which gave us an output of 85%. Scientists can develop a technology but Climber Enviro Solutions enables them to use it to make customized machines. Thus more scientists approached us to build customized machines based on their technology.

2008

Vigyan Ashram, an NGO had made a prototype of Rice De-Husking machine. We took this as a challenge and based on our design, we built a machine. Often building a machine on a large scale on a prototype is a tough task; after all, we had to make a fully operative and finished product from it. It involved selecting the correct material, perfect assembly and making it easy to operate. We observed that a farmer doesn’t require big machinery for his farm; what he needs is smaller machine that will give maximum output. In fact such small machines could start a revolution in the smaller pockets of India and develop future entrepreneurs who can become self sufficient. Thus we made our first Rice de-husking machine which gave us a vision that if we provide them to the farmers and self help groups, it would make value addition to their lives. The Rice De-Husking machine was launched, but we didn’t stop at only building it. We acquired a certification of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) for this machine, so the farmers could get subsidy to purchase it. This machine has a subsidy of 50% all over India. Thus our journey of design, manufacturing and takingit to the farmer was fulfilled. Our new Groundnut peeling machine can peel allied lentils also. We are working on a Rice Thresher which will separate Rice from its cover. This year we plan to bring a series of such Agriculture centric machines.
